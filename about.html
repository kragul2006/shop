Our Story

Born from a Passion for Home & Comfort

Sri Lakshmi Multi Shop was founded in 2017 in Puducherry with a simple mission — to offer thoughtfully selected home essentials that bring comfort, style, and utility to everyday life.

What began as a small neighbourhood shop has grown into a trusted local destination for cookware, bedding, decor, and smart storage solutions. We curate products from reliable makers and brands to ensure value and durability.

Every product in our collection is chosen for its quality and usefulness. When you shop with us, you bring home items designed to last and to make daily life a little easier.

Our Journey

2017
The Beginning
Sri Lakshmi Multi Shop opens its doors in White Town, Puducherry, with a small collection of curated home essentials and cookware.
2018
Growing Trust
Expanded our collection to include kitchenware and home decor. Reached 500+ loyal customers in our first year.
2021
Digital Presence
Launched our online catalogue to serve customers beyond Puducherry.
2024
5000+ Happy Families
Celebrated serving over 5000 families. Expanded our bedding and storage categories by popular demand.
Today
Still Growing, Still Caring
With 500+ products and a team that treats every customer like family, we continue to serve with the same love and passion as day one.
